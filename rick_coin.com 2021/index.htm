BUy back & burn mechanism

Rick coin has a unique burn mechanism.
Indeed, we have access to liquidity, this liquidity is composed of BNB and RICK COIN.
Every day, we will withdraw 20% of this liquidity and use it to buyback and burn RICK COIN tokens.
All proofs of buy and burn will of course be posted on our social networks.
We hope that this mechanism will help the project to last through time compared to other similar tokens.
