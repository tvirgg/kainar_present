Louvre

In October 2019, Paris’ Louvre launched ‘Mona Lisa: Beyond the Glass’, a VR experience that explores the Renaissance painting as part of its Leonardo da Vinci blockbuster exhibition. Through interactive design, sound and animated images, users discover details about the painting, such as its wood panel texture and how the passage of time has changed the way it looks

Available in five languages, the experience can be enjoyed for four months by booking directly at the Louvre and is downloadable on VR app store VIVEPORT, iOS, and Android.
